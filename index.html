    • About Me

    Hi there! I am Ronda, a two-year-old Labrador Retriever who started training to become a guide dog for the blind when I was little. I am very friendly and energetic puppy, always eager to learn new things and meet new people and receiving new toys. With my intelligence, loyalty, and gentle nature (and cuteness of course),I have all the traits necessary to become a fantastic guide dog. This is why when I was one-year-old I passed all the tests to become a guide dog. Actually, I did so well that they decided they want to keep my genes, so my puppies will be guide dogs as well and provide invaluable assistance to those with visual impairments. So now, I am a mother of 9 little puppies who are undergoing their training.
